# 🧠 Meeting Bullshit Counter

Meetings consume valuable time, but it is often difficult to measure whether they actually produce meaningful results.

**Meeting Bullshit Counter** takes a meeting transcript as input and analyzes it to provide insights such as:

- 🔁 Repetition level of the discussion
- 🗣️ Amount of filler language used
- ✅ Actionable outcomes generated
- 📊 Overall meeting productivity score

The service provides measurable feedback that helps teams understand and improve their meeting efficiency.
